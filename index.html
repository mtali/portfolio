About me

Hi, I'm Emmanuel, a mobile engineer based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. I've spent the last 8 years building Android apps that hold up in the field, on weak networks, in the hands of people doing real work.

I lead the mobile platform at Jaza Energy, a solar company powering off-grid communities across Tanzania and Nigeria. I founded our two production apps, migrated our core app from Java to Kotlin, and re-architected it into clean, multi-module Compose. Before Jaza I built the Tanzania payments side of MyDStv (5M+ downloads) at Mobile Power.

I work across the stack too, Spring Boot and Laravel backends, so I understand both ends of the wire. Here are a few technologies I work with:

  • Kotlin
  • Jetpack Compose
  • Kotlin Multiplatform
  • Coroutines & Flow
  • Spring Boot
  • Laravel / PHP
